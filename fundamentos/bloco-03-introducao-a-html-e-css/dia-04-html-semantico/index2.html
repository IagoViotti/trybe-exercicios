Some facts about Stomatopoda

General Information

The Mantis Shrimp (Odontodactylus scyllarus), ou stomatopods, are carnivorous marine crustaceans. They belong in the animal kingdom , phylum Arthropoda and Crustacea subphylum, class Malacostraca, subclass Hoplocarida and the order Stomotopoda.

Their Eyes

Their eyes contain millions of light sensitive cells, called rods and cones. Rods enable them to see light and motion. Cones enable them to see color. When it comes to vision, the mantis are top of the food chain, above the butterflies whom has favi tipes of color-receptive cones, they have incredible sixteen color-receptive cones.

Their Strenght

Even though they are incredibly colorful they apeared to be one of the most creatively violent creature on the planet. It has two raptorial appendages on the front of it's body wich accelerate with the same velocity as a gunshot from a twenty-two caliber rifle and in less than three-thousandths of a second can strike prey with 1.500 Newtons of force.
